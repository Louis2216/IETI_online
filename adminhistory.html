Our History

IETI College of Science and Technology, San Pedro was established in 1996. It offers technical courses in ITM, CTSM, EST, MEET, and AET, as well as baccalaureate degrees in BS Information Technology and BS in Industrial Technology with majors in Electronics, Industry Electricity Computer, Refrigeration, Airconditioning, and Automotive.

Our Mission

We produce graduates with internationally recognized certifications, equipping them with the knowledge, technical skills, and attitude to excel in their chosen fields.

Our Vision

To be the best training institution in Asia Pacific, known for the knowledge, technical skills, and attitude of our faculty, students, and graduates.
